### What is Scout?

With Scout, we're transforming the web into a permissionless database. Scout captures public web traffic, allowing us to build datasets in the open data marketplace that fulfill key enterprise needs.

### How do I get started?

Download the [Extension](https://app.chakra.dev/). Browse the internet as your normally would and climb the leaderboard.
