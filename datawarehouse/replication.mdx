## Intro

Chakra currently supports replcations via the following sources and destinations:

- BigQuery -> GCS
- GCS -> S3
- Snowflake -> S3
- S3 -> S3

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title='Create a Replication'>
    Navigate to [Replications](https://console.chakra.dev/replication) and click
    on the "Create Replication" button.
    <img src='/images/replication-1.png' />
  </Step>
  <Step title='Name the Replication'>
    Give the replication a name that you can identify it by.
    <img src='/images/replication-2.png' />
  </Step>
  <Step title='Create a Source'>
    Create a source via the dropdown (or choose a previously created source).
    <img src='/images/replication-3.png' />
    Enter details as necessary.
    <img src='/images/replication-6.png' />
  </Step>
  <Step title='Create a Destination'>
    Similarly, create or choose a destination via the dropdown.
    <img src='/images/replication-7.png' />
    Enter details as necessary.
    <img src='/images/replication-8.png' />
  </Step>
  <Step title='Save Replication'>
    Save the replication, and navigate to the replication page.
    <img src='/images/replication-11.png' />
  </Step>
  <Step title='Create a Table'>
    Click on the "Add Table" button, and enter details as necessary.
    <img src='/images/replication-12.png' />
  </Step>
  <Step title='Trigger Sync'>
    Click on "Sync Now" in the "Sync" dropdown.
    <img src='/images/replication-14.png' />
    Choose whether you would like an incremental or full sync. The sync can also
    be triggered via tha [API](/api-reference/run-table-sync).
    <img src='/images/replication-15.png' />
  </Step>
  <Step title='Verify Sync Result'>
    After the sync is finished, click on the Sync History to see its final
    status.
    <img src='/images/replication-17.png' />
  </Step>
</Steps>
